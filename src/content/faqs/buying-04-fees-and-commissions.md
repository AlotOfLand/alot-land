No. We sell directly — there are no realtor commissions, no surprise fees, and no runaround. The price listed is the price you pay, plus standard closing costs (title, deed recording) which we're transparent about upfront. We work with a licensed title company to handle the paperwork and ensure a clean transfer of ownership.
