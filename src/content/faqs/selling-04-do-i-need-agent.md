question: Do I need a real estate agent to sell my land to you?
category: selling
order: 9
active: true

No. You work directly with us — no agent required and no commission coming out of your pocket. Realtor commissions on land sales typically run 6–10%, which on a $50,000 property is $3,000–$5,000 gone before you see a dime. We handle all the paperwork and use a licensed title company to make sure the transfer is clean and legal. You keep more of what your land is worth.
