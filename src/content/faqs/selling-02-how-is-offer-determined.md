---
question: How do you determine what to offer for my land?
category: selling
order: 7
active: true
---

We look at recent comparable sales in your area, the property's access, utilities, zoning, size, and its potential for subdivision or resale. We do our homework before making an offer. Our goal is a fair number that works for both sides — we're not here to lowball. We want to build long-term relationships with landowners, and that starts with being straight with you on value.
